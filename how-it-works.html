How It Works

We use automation to speed up sourcing and screening for lower priority construction searches, then review candidates before delivery. Sometimes you need a luxury car. Sometimes you need a work truck. We're the work truck.

1

Submit Your Search Requirements

Complete our simple intake form with your lower priority search requirements:

  • - Position title and department
  • - Salary range and location
  • - Required experience and skills
  • - Timeline and priority level
  • - Company information and context

Perfect For:

  • - Assistant Project Managers
  • - Junior Estimators
  • - Site Supervisors
  • - Safety Coordinators
  • - Office Managers
  • - Supporting roles ($45-80K range)
2

Automation Helps With Sourcing

After intake, we use automation to help move the search forward:

  • - Organizes search details from the intake form
  • - Helps sort and screen incoming candidate information
  • - Flags likely matches based on role, location, and pay
  • - Keeps the process moving without unnecessary meetings
  • - Supports faster handoff into human review

Transparent Process:

We are direct about this: automation helps with speed and organization, but clients should still expect human review before candidate delivery.

3

Human Quality Review

Before delivering candidates, our team reviews the search output:

  • - Reviews candidate information manually
  • - Checks fit against the search requirements
  • - Confirms location, compensation, and role alignment
  • - Screens out obvious misses before delivery
  • - Delivers candidates only after review

Quality Promise:

The goal is simple: use automation where it saves time, then keep a human in the loop before anything is sent to the client.

4

Receive Your Candidates

Once the search is ready, you receive a reviewed candidate package:

  • - A short list of reviewed candidates
  • - Candidate profiles and experience summaries
  • - Contact information and availability
  • - Compensation context and fit notes
  • - Clear next-step direction

Delivery Expectations:

Timing depends on the role, market, and feedback cycle. The point is a simpler process and less drag than a traditional search.

5

Interview & Hire

You take it from here. We do the heavy lifting up front:

  • - Interview your top candidate choices
  • - Make offers to your preferred candidates
  • - Complete your hiring process
  • - Pay our $5K fee only when you hire
  • - No fee if we do not deliver on the first search

Payment Terms:

$5,000 flat fee paid only upon successful hire.

First search is free if we do not deliver. Every successful placement includes a 30-day replacement guarantee.
