Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the first search free?

We're confident in our system. The free search lets you experience our quality and process with zero risk. Most clients come back for more searches after seeing the results.

What if none of your candidates work out?

You pay nothing. Our fee is only charged when you successfully hire one of our candidates. If our candidates don't meet your standards, we learn from the feedback and improve.

How do you deliver candidates so fast?

AI automation handles the time-consuming parts (posting, screening, initial qualification) while humans focus on quality review. This lets us deliver in 5-7 days instead of weeks.

Is this only for construction companies?

Currently yes. We specialize in construction industry recruiting because we understand the unique challenges, seasonal patterns, and skill requirements. This focus makes us more effective.

What size companies do you work with?

We're perfect for construction companies with 20-200 employees who need 2-5 lower priority searches per year. Large enough to need professional help, small enough to appreciate our efficient approach.

Do you handle senior executive searches?

No. For VP, C-level, or other critical leadership roles, you need the luxury car approach with executive search firms. We're the work truck - perfect for supporting roles that keep your business running.
