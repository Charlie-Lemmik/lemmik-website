Our Core Values

1. Radical Transparency

We don't fake it. If we're new, we say we're new. If we use AI to find candidates, we tell you. No "proprietary black box" nonsense. Just honest expectations.

2. Work Truck Efficiency

No chrome, no polish, just payload. We value results over presentation. We don't do unnecessary Zoom meetings. We respect your time by operating asynchronously.

3. Diamonds First

We don't sell what we don't have. Quality candidates are the currency; everything else is noise. We build the pipeline before we ask for your business.

4. Simple is Strong

Flat fees. Simple contracts. Digital processes. If it's complicated, we cut it. Recruiting shouldn't require a legal team to sign an agreement.
